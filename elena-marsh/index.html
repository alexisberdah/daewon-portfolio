MY STORY

I burned out so you don't have to.

Ten years ago, I was a corporate strategist pulling 70-hour weeks, collecting promotions, and slowly losing myself. I had the career everyone envied — and the anxiety, insomnia, and emptiness nobody saw.

That breakdown became my breakthrough. I retrained as a coach, left the corner office, and spent the next decade helping over 500 women build careers that actually light them up — without sacrificing their health, relationships, or sanity.

Today I run a practice that reaches clients in 12 countries, host the Lit From Within podcast (1M+ downloads), and still get excited for Monday mornings.
