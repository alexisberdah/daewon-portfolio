ABOUT THE STUDIO

Architecture is the thoughtful making of space.

Founded in 2011, Hale & Stone is an award-winning architecture and design studio based in London. We believe that great architecture emerges from a deep understanding of place, people, and purpose.

Our work spans residential, cultural, and commercial projects across Europe and Asia. Every project begins with listening — to the site, to the client, and to the light.
